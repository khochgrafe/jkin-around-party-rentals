Rental Information

🚚 Delivery Options

Every rental includes the option of:

✔ Delivery with customer setup/takedown (instructions provided)

OR

✔ Delivery, professional setup and takedown by JK'in Around Party Rentals.

⚡ Power Requirements

Extension cords are not provided. Customers must provide an outdoor electrical outlet within reach of the inflatable.

A signed liability waiver is required before any equipment is used. Customers accept responsibility for safe supervision and understand JK'in Around Party Rentals is not responsible for injuries, accidents or misuse of rented equipment.

Frequently Asked Questions

How long is a rental?

Daily rentals are for one full day. Multi-day rentals are available upon request.

What if it rains?

Weather happens! Contact us and we'll work with you to reschedule whenever possible.

Who sets everything up?

You can choose customer setup with instructions or professional setup and takedown by our team.

Is a waiver required?

Yes. Every rental requires a signed liability waiver before equipment can be used.
