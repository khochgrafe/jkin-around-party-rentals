Frequently Asked Questions

How far in advance should I book?

We recommend booking early, especially for weekends and holiday dates. Availability is limited and rentals are reserved on a first-come basis.

Do you provide setup?

Yes! Customers can choose self-setup instructions or add professional setup and takedown service for an additional fee.

What happens to my deposit?

Your security deposit is returned once the rental equipment has been inspected and confirmed to be returned without damage.

Do I need to sign anything?

Yes. Every rental requires a signed rental agreement and liability waiver before equipment is delivered or picked up.
